About This Event

Join 2,000+ AI practitioners, researchers, and business leaders at the premier artificial intelligence conference of 2026. Explore the latest breakthroughs in machine learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and responsible AI.

What You'll Learn

  • State-of-the-art techniques in large language models and foundation models
  • Real-world case studies of AI deployment at scale across industries
  • Hands-on workshops with leading AI frameworks and tools
  • Strategies for responsible AI development and governance
  • Networking opportunities with top AI researchers and engineers

Who Should Attend

This event is ideal for AI/ML engineers, data scientists, product managers, CTOs, researchers, and anyone interested in the future of intelligent systems. Whether you're building AI-powered products or exploring how AI can transform your organization, this summit has something for you.

Why Attend?

Get exclusive access to pre-release demos, participate in hands-on labs, and connect with peers from companies like Google, Meta, OpenAI, Microsoft, and startups building the next generation of AI applications.
